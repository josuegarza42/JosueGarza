Depsthink

We realized that mental health education in Mexico is deficient. Depsthink will be an app that will bring the patient closer to the psychologist, the patient will be able to consult safe information about a mental illness, the user will also be able to contact a psychologist through the app and make an appointment. We would like to implement an AI so that the user can do a test in the app which will provide a non definitive result, the AI can prediagnose a mental illness and refer him to the psychologist that best suits him.
